Admin

The Admin module allows system administrator to control all the business processes and manage all users, groups and operations of the application.

Sub Modules
Dashboard Can View dashbaords of all end users.
Customer Manage Customers
Course Manage Courses
Subscription Manage Subscription
Payment View all payment history
Report Generate Reports

Dashboard

The Dashboard module allows admin to view dashboard of sales, customer and course.

Dashboard View:
Customer Dashboard

The customer dashboard shows the analytics data of customers.

Fields Description Image
Total Shows total number of customers
New Shows number of new customers
Repeat Shows number of repeat customers
Drop Shows number of drop customers
LTV Shows LTV of customers of last three years in graph form
Social Shows customers ratio based on social profiles like facebbok, google and instagram
Active Shows list of top active customers
Device Shows list of customers by device
Country Shows list of customers by country
Sessions Shows list of customer sessions by country and sessions number
New Per Month Shows graph of new customers by month
Drop off Graph Shows drop off of customers by month
Sales Dashboard

The Sales dashboard shows the sales ratio data through pictorial representation.

Customer

The Customer module allows admin to manage customer details. Allows admin to view , edit , create , delete and export customers.

Customer Manual

The manual will help user to guide with instructions.

Steps to Add Customer
Step Description Interface
1 Click on Add Customer Button
2 Fill out details in form
3 Click on Add Customer to add customer details
Steps to Edit Customer
Step Description Interface
1 Click on Pencil icon
2 Change details in form
3 Click on Update button to change customer details
Steps to Export Customer
Step Description Interface
1 Click on Export button
2 Click on Download button to export customer
Steps to Delete Customer

Admin can delete customer by following two ways

Method 1 Step Description Interface
1 Click on Delete icon
2 Click on Yes, Delete it! button to delete customer
Method 2 Step Description Interface
1 Select customers you want to remove from list
2 Click on Delete icon to delete customers
Steps to View Customer

From View customer admin can see all the activities of customer like their personal details, interests, activities, courses, subscriptions, certificates, payments, reviews and devices.

Step Description Interface
1 Click on view icon
2 Can see the Overview of customer
3 To view Customer Activities click on Activities
3 To view Customer Course click on Courses
3 To view Customer Subscriptions click on Subscription
3 To view Customer Certificates click on Certificate
3 To view Customer Payment history click on Payment
3 To view Customer Review click on Review
3 To view Customer Devices click on Devices
4 To view Customer Certificate click on icon and View From option
4 To download Customer Certificate click on icon and Download From option
